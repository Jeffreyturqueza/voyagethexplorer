ABOUT US.

VoyageTheExplorer, a land-based tourism site based in the Philippines, was initiated to highlight the under-the-radar, under-explored spots around Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. It was conceived out of a collective love for slow, earthed travels—one that appreciates the journey as much as the destination.

Founded by passionate travelers, the platform highlights accessible road travel, offering information on peaceful mountain towns, historic sites, cultural events, and scenic drives. It also provides travel information, insider knowledge, and green travel advice.
